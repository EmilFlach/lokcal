Features

Simple to use.
Smart over time.

No subscriptions, no account. Your food database starts small and grows to fit how you actually eat.

📊
See your week at a glance

The main screen shows a kcal graph for the full week. Spot a heavy day early and adjust — before it becomes a pattern.

📈
Learn your actual limits

Analytics build over time to show how many kcal you can eat without gaining or losing weight — personalised to you, not a generic formula.

🛒
Data from where you shop

Connect to Albert Heijn, Esselunga, or Kroger and pull kcal data from the exact products you buy. Open Food Facts covers anything else.

⚡
Fast enough to disappear

Log a full meal in under 10 seconds. Once something is in your database, search results are instant — no network, no spinner, just go.
