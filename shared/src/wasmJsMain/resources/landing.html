Features

Just calories.
Gets out of your way.

🔒
Your data never leaves your phone

No cloud means no account, no breach, and nothing to sell.

🛒
Food data that matches what you actually buy

90% of what you eat is already local — for the rest, search your actual store and get a few relevant results, not hundreds of duplicates.

📈
Learn your actual maintenance calories

Watches your real weight against your real intake and works out your actual number — no questionnaire.

⚡
1–2 seconds per item

Everything is on-device, so you're in and out in seconds — on a normal day you'll spend half the time you would in a cloud-based app.

Also included
  • Barcode scanner
  • "Same as yesterday" shortcut
  • Leftover and frequent item shortcuts
  • Weekly dashboard — rebalance today to hit your goals
  • Food breakdown — see what's costing you the most
  • Weight & exercise tracking
  • Import and export your data
